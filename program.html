Our Programs

Pre-University (PUC)

Students are encouraged to explore real-life scenarios, such as designing experiments to understand scientific principles or analyzing case studies to grasp business and economic concepts. Hegde Institutes caters to students aiming to excel in their pre-university studies, providing programs in:

1st PUC and 2nd PUC - Science Stream

Subjects offered include Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology/Computer Science. The curriculum is designed to build a strong foundation for competitive exams like NEET, JEE, and CET, ensuring students are well-prepared for future academic challenges.

+

1st PUC and 2nd PUC - Commerce Stream

Subjects include Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Statistics. The program aims to nurture future professionals with a focus on building strong analytical and business skills.

+

Postgraduate Programs

Postgraduate students engage in research, projects, and practical applications to tackle industry-relevant problems in fields like physics and mathematics. Hegde Institutes also offers advanced academic programs for postgraduate students, specializing in:

M.Sc. Physics

This program provides in-depth knowledge of advanced physics concepts, fostering research and innovation. The curriculum is meticulously structured to cater to students aspiring for careers in academia, research, or industry roles in physics.

+

M.Sc. Mathematics

Focused on advanced mathematical theories and their practical applications, this program is ideal for students aiming to pursue careers in data science, actuarial science, research, or academia.
